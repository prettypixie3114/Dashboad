Section J · non-gold KPIs · phase 1 targets
The brutally honest read on this dashboard
01
You're losing money slower, not making it. Contribution margin −₹1.84L MTD beats Mar-Apr's run-rate of −₹6.5L/mo but you're still bleeding. The Day 30 target of −₹2L to +₹3L is achievable; +₹3L would require an extra 30 bundle orders in the next 15 days. Push C7 and C9 budgets — both are above their ROAS targets.
02
Discount % is the single biggest leak. At 17.4% you're still effectively 5pp above the Phase 1 ceiling. Audit: is the PIXIE10 stack happening on top of existing SKU markdowns? If yes, that's where margin is leaking. Either kill stacking at checkout or accept that "10% prepaid" is a permanent discount, not a prepaid incentive.
03
Click-to-session loss is bigger than your media problem. 63% of paid clicks never become sessions. Even the 40% target wastes ₹2.7L/mo of ad spend in transit. Before scaling spend, fix LP load speed, mobile checkout drawer, and pixel deduplication. Otherwise every campaign optimization here is sandcastle work.
04
C9 Reactivation is your only 5x campaign. Predicted 3.5–4.5x, actual 4.12x. This is the path to blended 5x MER — but only if the past-buyer pool grows. Today's pool is finite. Every C1/C2 cold purchase you make today becomes C9 inventory in 60 days. Stop treating C9 as a stand-alone campaign; treat it as the destination for C1/C2/C7.
05
C5 LAL is approaching the death spiral you escaped from. Frequency 2.9x with refresh trigger at 3.5x and the prior account hit 5.39x. Auto-refresh the seed audience THIS WEEK, don't wait for the 30-day MCP automation to fire.
06
Bundle attach at 6.4% means the upsell mechanic isn't visible enough. Target is 25–35% by Day 60. Audit: is the Core Pair offer above-the-fold on PDPs? In the cart drawer? In post-add-to-cart modals? If the only place customers see "Get 2 for ₹7,999" is the ad — you're missing 80% of the touchpoint surface.
07
What this dashboard cannot tell you yet: repeat purchase rate is still establishing baseline. Until you have ≥60 days of post-purchase data, every LTV number here is a model, not an observation. Don't make CAC payback decisions on the LTV trend line until Day 75+.
